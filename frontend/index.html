Our Mission

Empowering Youth to Prevent Crime and Substance Abuse

The African Youth Initiative on Crime Prevention (AYICRIP) recognizes that real solutions require continental cooperation. This first-of-its-kind summit creates a platform where young leaders connect with policymakers, enforcement, and communities.

Our focus: prevention strategies, grassroots health programs, cross-border collaboration, and digital solutions tailored to African communities.

Summit Goals

What We Aim to Achieve

Raise Awareness

Understand the root causes of drug distribution and youth vulnerability through evidence-based approaches.

Empower Youth

Equip young leaders with governance skills, grant management, and peer advocacy training.

Shape Policy

Draft a unified Youth Action Manifesto on drug and crime prevention for national legislation.

Build Partnerships

Connect advocacy groups, law enforcement, and local communities for lasting impact.

Use Technology

Deploy digital reporting tools, spatial mapping, and mental health apps for prevention.

Community Action

Launch sustainable community safety programs owned and managed by local youth groups.
